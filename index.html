About me

More than just design.

I'm naturally curious and love understanding how people think, behave, and interact with technology. Design gives me the opportunity to turn that curiosity into meaningful experiences that solve real problems. I enjoy simplifying complex systems, refining details that improve usability, and collaborating with people who care deeply about building thoughtful products.

Outside of work, I deeply enjoy yoga for the sense of calm and grounding it brings me, and I love surrounding myself with plants — although keeping them alive consistently is still very much a work in progress.

When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me planning a trip, discovering a new café, volunteering, or binge-watching tv shows.
